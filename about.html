Company History

Operational Timeline

2018
ShieldGuard Founded
Marcus Chen launches ShieldGuard from a small research lab in San Francisco. Initial focus on AI-driven threat detection algorithms for financial institutions.
2019
First SOC Deployed
Opened our first Security Operations Center in Virginia. Secured first 50 enterprise clients including 3 Fortune 500 companies. Team grows to 75 employees.
2020
Global Expansion
Opened European HQ in London and APAC hub in Singapore. Launched cloud security division. Protected clients through 340% increase in pandemic-related cyber attacks.
2021
Zero Trust Platform Launch
Released ShieldGuard Zero Trust Platform. Achieved SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications. Blocked over 500 million threats for the first time.
2022
AI Threat Engine V3
Launched next-gen AI threat detection engine with 99.97% accuracy. Secured FedRAMP authorization. Grew to 350+ employees across 5 global SOCs.
2023
Critical Infrastructure Division
Formed dedicated critical infrastructure protection unit. Partnered with government agencies for national security initiatives. Surpassed 1 billion threats blocked.
2024
Industry Recognition
Named a Leader in Gartner Magic Quadrant for MDR. Reached 500+ employees, 3,200+ clients, and 7 global SOC locations. $100M ARR milestone.
2025-26
Quantum-Ready Security
Pioneering post-quantum cryptography solutions. Launched quantum-safe encryption toolkit. Preparing enterprise clients for the post-quantum threat era.
